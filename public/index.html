Frequently Asked Questions

What is FraudShield?

FraudShield is a mobile security app designed specifically for India. It protects you from scam SMS and phishing emails by using a combination of keyword detection, link analysis, and a powerful crowdsourced database of known fraud schemes.

How does reporting a scam help?

When you report a scam, the fraudulent number or email address is added to our central database. This information is then used to warn and protect all other FraudShield users in real-time. Your one-tap action creates a stronger shield for millions.

Is my personal data safe?

Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy. The app only scans for scam-related patterns and does not read your personal messages or emails. Identity verification data is encrypted and used solely for the purpose of creating a trusted user community to fight fraud.

Why do you need identity verification?

Identity verification helps prevent misuse of the reporting system. By ensuring our users are real people, we can trust the scam reports and act on them faster. This creates a highly reliable and effective community-driven defense system.
